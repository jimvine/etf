Enter the costs associated with the evaluation, broken down however makes sense for your project.

One option is to break the costs down by time period, such as by years or by quarters. In this case the cost items could simply be 'Evaluation costs in year 1', 'Evaluation costs in year 2', etc.

Another option is to break the costs down by types of activity, for example adding cost items for 'Evaluation fieldwork', 'Evaluation analysis', etc.

If you just want to record the total cost of the evaluation, enter 'Whole evaluation' as the item and then the total cost as the associated cost.
